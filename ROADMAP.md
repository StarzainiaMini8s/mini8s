# AS OF v0.3.99.2

Here will be the roadmap for Mini8s, going out with 1 patches and 1 major update into the future.
Shown here will be the planned features and patches for the next versions of Mini8s!

**v0.4.0**
- **LOCAL FORECAST MODE WITH TOGGLEABLE VOICE NARRATION (done by me)**
- **Significantly improved loading times, already implemented in dev builds.**
- Graphical overhaul, currently named "Cobalt"
- Potential new LDL re-design away from the modified IntelliSTAR LDLs.
- Two new choosable themes along with the current known theme (Cobalt)
- Legacy binary for older Windows computers.
- (MAYBE) Android app that hooks into a running instance of Mini8s and sends the user notifications on their phone.
- Drag-and-drop music support, can either be looped indefinitely or shuffled.
